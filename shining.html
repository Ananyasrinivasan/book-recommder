🪓 Welcome to the Overlook Hotel! 🏨

Where the hallways are *too long*, the rooms have *too much history*, and **your dad might go insane**. 🌨️😱

📖 Summary (With Extra Creeps!)

Jack Torrance takes his family to **The Overlook Hotel** for a peaceful winter getaway. Except... the hotel is *not empty*. The ghosts are *not friendly*. And Jack? Well, let’s just say he’s **not winning Father of the Year**. 🪓😵

🌍 World & Characters

  • 🪓 **Jack Torrance** – Aspiring writer, recovering alcoholic, and the guy you *don’t* want chasing you through a maze.
  • 👦 **Danny Torrance** – A kid with psychic powers (*The Shining*), who sees things he *really* shouldn't. 🔪
  • 👩 **Wendy Torrance** – Just trying to survive *both* ghosts and her husband.
  • 🏨 **The Overlook Hotel** – A haunted hotel with *really bad reviews* on Yelp.
  • 👻 **The Twins** – "Come play with us, Danny... forever... and ever..." 😱

🏆 Why You Should Read It

✔️ If you love **psychological horror and haunted hotels**. 🏨
✔️ If you want a **terrifying lesson in “family vacations gone wrong”**. 🪓
✔️ If you enjoy reading something that will make you **leave the lights on at night**. 😨
