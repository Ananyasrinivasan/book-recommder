🦇 Welcome to *Dracula* – Beware the Bite! 🧛‍♂️

⚰️ Step into the shadows... where the night belongs to **Count Dracula**.

📖 Summary (With Extra Bites!)

Jonathan Harker visits **Castle Dracula** in Transylvania for a real estate deal. Turns out, his client is **not your average count** – he drinks blood, controls wolves, and doesn’t reflect in mirrors. 🪞😱 Can Harker and his friends stop Dracula before he turns **London into his personal buffet**?

🌍 World & Characters

  • 🧛‍♂️ **Count Dracula** – The original bloodsucker. Prefers his meals... *extra rare*.
  • 🕵️ **Van Helsing** – A vampire hunter who’s had *enough* of Dracula’s nonsense.
  • 📜 **Jonathan Harker** – Just a guy who wanted to sell a house… not fight evil.
  • 💋 **Mina & Lucy** – Women dealing with *major* vampire problems.
  • 🐺 **Wolves & Bats** – Dracula’s personal delivery service. 🦇

🏆 Why You Should Read It

✔️ If you love **gothic horror, castles, and questionable real estate deals**. 🏰
✔️ If you enjoy **charming yet terrifying villains**. 🦇
✔️ If you want to know where **every vampire story started**. 🩸
