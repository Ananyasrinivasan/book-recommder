V 🔎 The Da Vinci Code

🔎 Welcome to *The Da Vinci Code* – A Book of Secrets!

Get ready for puzzles, secret societies, and the realization that maybe you should’ve paid more attention in history class. 🏛️

📖 Summary (With Extra Mystery)

Professor Robert Langdon + A Cryptologist + A Murder in the Louvre = A wild adventure that questions everything you thought you knew about history. 🧐💡

🌍 World & Characters

  • 📖 **Robert Langdon** – A Harvard symbologist who wears way too much tweed.
  • 🕵️‍♀️ **Sophie Neveu** – A cryptologist with family secrets deeper than the Louvre.
  • 🛡️ **The Priory of Sion** – A secret society that might just know *too much*.
  • 🎨 **The Mona Lisa** – Who knew she was hiding *even more* secrets?

🏆 Why You Should Read It

✔️ It's basically a *National Treasure* movie but with way more history and Latin. 🏰
✔️ You'll feel like a genius every time you understand a clue. 🧠✨
✔️ You can impress people at parties by randomly mentioning Fibonacci numbers. 🔢
