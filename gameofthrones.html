⚔️ Welcome to Westeros! 🐉

"When you play the game of thrones, you win or you die." – *No pressure, right?* 👀

📖 Summary (With Betrayals!)

People **fight over an uncomfortable-looking chair** while a **dragon queen, ice zombies, and lots of family drama** unfold. 🏰🔥❄️ Don't get attached to anyone – because **they might not be alive by the next chapter.**

🌍 World & Characters

  • 🦁 **Tyrion Lannister** – Drinks wine, makes sarcastic comments, somehow *the smartest one here.*
  • 🐺 **Jon Snow** – Broods 80% of the time. *Knows nothing.*
  • 🐉 **Daenerys Targaryen** – Owns dragons. Would rather burn cities than negotiate. 🔥
  • 🦁 **Cersei Lannister** – Loves power, hates almost everyone, and is *scarily good* at politics.
  • 🏰 **Eddard Stark** – **Too honorable for this world.** Seriously.
  • ☠️ **The Night King** – Enjoys cold weather, raising the dead, and ruining everyone's plans.

🏆 Why You Should Read It

✔️ If you enjoy **political backstabbing, swords, and an extreme lack of plot armor.**
✔️ If you like books where **dragons and zombies coexist in the same universe.** 🐉❄️
✔️ If you're ready for **one of the most detailed, epic, and occasionally painful stories ever written.**

⭐ Fun Rating: "5/5 Beheadings!" ⚔️⚔️⚔️⚔️⚔️

(*Warning: May cause attachment issues and an obsession with medieval politics. 👑)
