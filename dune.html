"He who controls the spice, controls the universe!" (And probably your caffeine addiction.) ☕

📖 Summary (but with humor! 🤣)

A young noble, Paul Atreides, moves to a desert planet full of spice, giant worms, and betrayal. He goes from emo teenager to space messiah in record time. Think *Game of Thrones*, but with way more sand. 🏜️

🌍 World & Characters

  • 🏜️ **Arrakis** – The driest planet ever. SPF 10,000 recommended.
  • 🐛 **Sandworms** – They eat people. They poop spice. What a life.
  • 👑 **Paul Atreides** – Main character. Future space god. No pressure.
  • 👵 **Bene Gesserit** – Space nuns who manipulate genetics. Also, creepy whispers.
  • 💀 **Baron Harkonnen** – Villain. Floats. Loves evil monologues.
