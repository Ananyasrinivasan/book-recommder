🌌 DON’T PANIC! 🌌

"The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy" is here to help (or confuse) you! 🤯

📖 Summary (but with humor! 🤣)

Arthur Dent wakes up, finds out Earth is about to be demolished, and suddenly hitchhikes across the universe with a towel and a depressed robot. Welcome to space, buddy. 🛸

🌍 World & Characters

  • 🌍 **Earth** – Gone. Oops.
  • 👽 **Ford Prefect** – A chill alien who thinks cars are people.
  • 💡 **Zaphod Beeblebrox** – Two heads, three arms, zero common sense.
  • 🤖 **Marvin** – The most depressed robot ever.
  • 🪐 **The Universe** – Big. Weird. Full of restaurants.

🏆 Why You Should Read It

✔️ British humor at its finest 🇬🇧
✔️ An actual guide to intergalactic survival 📖
✔️ Deep philosophical insights (or not) 🧐
✔️ Teaches you why **42** is the answer to life, the universe, and everything 🔢

⭐ Fun Rating: "5/5 Towels!"

(Because you should always carry a towel.)
