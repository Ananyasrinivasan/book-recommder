🎈 Welcome to *IT* – The Town of Nightmares

📖 Summary (But with Maximum Terror!)

In the town of **Derry, Maine**, kids start disappearing... one by one. A group of childhood friends must face their worst fears. But the real question is... **can they escape Pennywise the Clown?** 🤡💀 *(Spoiler: Probably not.)*

🌍 World & Characters

  • 🤡 **Pennywise the Dancing Clown** – Wants to be your friend. (*RUN!*)
  • 🧒 **The Losers' Club** – A group of kids trying *not* to die.
  • 🏡 **Derry, Maine** – A cursed town where bad things happen. Always. 🌫️
  • 🎈 **Red Balloons** – If you see one... it's already too late. 🎈😱
