🧙‍♂️ Welcome to Hogwarts! ⚡

Wands ready! Swish, flick, and prepare for *unexpected deaths and snack-stealing house elves.*

📖 Summary (With Magic!)

Orphan boy finds out he’s a **wizard** and gets invited to a school where the teachers are either super wise or **high-key trying to kill him.** Oh, and there’s a *bald guy with no nose* who really hates him. 🎭

🌍 World & Characters

  • ⚡ **Harry Potter** – The chosen one. Has *chronic bad luck* and a scar that works as a Voldemort GPS.
  • 📖 **Hermione Granger** – The real hero. Would have solved *everything* in book one.
  • 🍗 **Ron Weasley** – Loyal best friend. Constantly hungry. Terrified of spiders.
  • 🐍 **Draco Malfoy** – Elite-level brat. Says *“Potter”* like it’s an insult.
  • 👨‍🏫 **Dumbledore** – Hogwarts' wise old headmaster, who gives cryptic advice instead of straight answers.
  • 🌑 **Voldemort** – Literally no nose. Obsessed with a teenager. Needs therapy.

🏆 Why You Should Read It

✔️ If you love **magic, adventure, and a school where detentions involve *actual life-threatening situations*.**
✔️ If you want a story where **love, friendship, and giant spiders coexist.** 🕷️
✔️ If you're into **fantasy worlds with talking hats and secret chambers.**

⭐ Fun Rating: "5/5 Butterbeers!" 🍻🍻🍻🍻🍻
