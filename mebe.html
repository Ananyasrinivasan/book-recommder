💔 Welcome to *Me Before You* – AKA "Prepare to Cry"

Love, loss, and life lessons that hit harder than a Nicholas Sparks novel. 😭

📖 Summary (With Extra Drama)

Quirky Lou meets brooding Will. He’s in a wheelchair; she’s in bright tights. A love story unfolds, but life (and gut-wrenching emotions) have other plans. 💘😭

🌍 World & Characters

  • 🌸 **Louisa Clark** – The human equivalent of sunshine in polka-dot dresses.
  • 🦽 **Will Traynor** – Former adrenaline junkie turned sarcasm king.
  • 💔 **A Love Story Like No Other** – Not your typical "happily ever after."
  • 🌍 **English Countryside** – The perfect backdrop for an emotional rollercoaster.

🏆 Why You Should Read It

✔️ It will make you laugh, cry, and reconsider your entire life. 🥲
✔️ Lou and Will’s banter is top-tier sarcasm. 🔥
✔️ It reminds you to live boldly (before you run out of time). ⏳

⭐ Fun Rating: "5/5 Tear-Soaked Tissues!"

(This book will emotionally wreck you... but in a good way. Probably.)
